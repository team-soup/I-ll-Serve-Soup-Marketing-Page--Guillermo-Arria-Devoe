About our Kitchen

Our Everybody Eats! Soup Kitchen provides hot, nutritious meals 365 days a year. There is no charge for the meals and no one hungry is ever turned away. We serve on average 200 meals a day and are very grateful for our dedicated volunteers!

Soup Kitchen Service Hours

Monday-Friday: 11:30AM - 1:00PM

Saturday: 11:30AM-12:30PM

Sunday: 2:00PM-3:00PM

VOLUNTEERS

Volunteers are important! Our soup kitchen relies on more than 1,000 volunteers annually. If you are looking to donate your time towards a greater good please reach out to us. We're also eager to take in student and government volunteers.

DONATIONS

In addition, all of the food we serve is donated from local restaurants, businesses, churches, civic groups and individuals. More than 65,000 pounds of food are donated each year to feed the hungry in our community!

DEDICATION

We are absolutely dedicated to running the best soup kitchen. Our kitchen is always kept stocked and organized because we use kitchen management software that allows us to track our inventory. When we need more food, our system reminds us to reach out.

"Our kitchen is dedicated to all those living in hunger today."

-Everybody Eats! Team
